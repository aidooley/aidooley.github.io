About Me

I am a sophomore graphic design student at The University of Massachusetts Dartmouth living in Chepachet Rhode Island. I want to specialize in packaging design and branding after graduating with my BFA. Besides art/design, I love yoga, bright colors, and cats. My artwork “Cheery Cherries” was featured in CVPA’s Emerging Young Artist’s art show and was awarded honorable mention. I’m currently accepting commissions for custom handpainted jackets, logo designs, and illustrations.
